Health Guide

Sunny Weather

  • Drink 3-4 litres of water
  • Apply sunscreen
  • Wear cotton clothes
  • Avoid direct sunlight

🌧 Rainy Weather

  • Carry an umbrella
  • Drink boiled water
  • Avoid stagnant water
  • Wear waterproof footwear

❄ Cold Weather

  • Wear warm clothes
  • Drink hot beverages
  • Eat protein-rich foods
  • Exercise daily
